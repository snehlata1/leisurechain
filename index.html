In a world full of exotic travel destinations and fascinating leisure experiences choosing the perfect place to travel can be daunting. With thousands of exciting experiences and adventures, how do you find the right one for you? Leisurechain.com is the world’s No.1 dedicated adventure and leisure operator’s hub featuring tailor made solutions for fun, excitement and special getaway experiences. The diverse range of activities on offer will satisfy the adventure needs of both young and old. Boutique tour operators featured among our pages can take you to some of the worlds best-kept secret holiday destinations and remote far away places off the beaten track.

Land – Sea – Air Travel Experience

Discover some of the worlds most exciting outback and safari destinations by taking a coach or private jeep tour. If hot air balloon rides, fishing cycling, hiking or a simple walking tour is your preference; you can be sure to find what you are looking for at leisurechain.com. For a leisurely way to savour a countries’ scenery, take a train journey or a relaxed sea cruise. These are great ways to view historical sites, ancient ruins and snow-capped mountains in comfort and style. If you prefer air travel, there are plenty of helicopter areal tours you can choose from

Thrill Seekers and Adrenaline Junkies

Adrenaline junkies can satisfy their need for extreme leisure pursuits by booking rafting and canoeing adventures in black and white waters, whilst thrill seekers can add amusement and theme parks to their travel itinerary. The young at heart can reconnect with the magic of yesteryear through the fascinating world of Disney, or the many thrilling and daredevil rides at the worlds best adventure parks. If you are planning a trip to a winter destination you may like to try snow skiing, snowboarding, ice climbing, mountaineering or join a dog sledding tour. Lovers of snow, can source the worlds most popular ski resorts, lodgings and chalets

Outback – Dessert – Yoga Mind and Body

Camping, glamping and caravanning can easily be incorporated into your safari or outback-themed holiday. Image lodging in a luxury desert camp in Australia’s spirited heartland! Or camping in some of the most isolated areas of Africa, where you are surrounded by the orange colour sand dunes, rugged mountains and rocky plains. If you prefer a quiet getaway experience, consider a yoga retreat to refresh the mind and rejuvenate the soul. Enjoy massages, scrubs and beauty treatment in pristine surroundings. Open your mind, body and senses to the wonderful world of mediation and relaxations retreats. For the super health conscious you can join one of the many detox, fitness and wellbeing programs on offer

Culinary - City and Historic Tours

Food, Brewery, Winery, City and Museum tours are a great way to get to know your holiday destination through its cuisine, people and landmarks. For those who love to shop, join a guided tour for an authentic retail therapy experience. Discover antique shops and art galleries, old and new museums, chat with locals and support their artisan producers.
